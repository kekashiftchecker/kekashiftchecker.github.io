Privacy Policy

  1. Data Collection: The Keka Shift Calculator extension collects token for fetching data related to your working hours, such as start and end times, break durations, and total working hours. This token is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties, except our website to show it on your other devices.
  2. Data Use: The data (token) collected by the Keka Shift Calculator extension is used solely for the purpose of calculating your working hours and providing you with accurate data. We do not use your data for any other purpose, and we do not share your data (token) with any third parties.
  3. Data Security: We take data security seriously and have implemented technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your data (token). However, we cannot guarantee the security of your data, and you use the Keka Shift Calculator extension at your own risk.
  4. Data Retention: The data (token) collected by the Keka Shift Calculator extension is stored locally on your device and is retained for as long as you use the extension. You can delete this data (token) at any time by uninstalling the extension or by loggin out from your keka web application so it the collected token will be expired.
  5. Changes to Privacy Policy: We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any updates.
  6. Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at keka.shift.calculator@gmail.com.
